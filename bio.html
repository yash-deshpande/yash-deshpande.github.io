Yash Deshpande

Yash Deshpande received a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay in 2011, and an MS and Ph.D in Electrical Engineering in 2016 from Stanford University. He is presently a Schramm fellow at the Department of Mathematics, MIT and Microsoft Research, New England. His research interests are in statistical inference, graphical models, message passing algorithms and applied probability.
